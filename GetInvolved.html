You do not have to be a coder to mentor at our club!

We benefit from a mix of Mentor's providing support, guidance, and encouragement to out Kodemokeies.

What's involved

How can I help

You can attend your Dojo’s meetings and help out for a couple of hours, whether that’s weekly, fortnightly or monthly.

Experienced coder

Help out at a Dojo and share your knowledge with local young people who are eager to learn!?

No technical expertise

No problem! We need awesome people to help with registration, updating social media, producing fliers etc. Any help is appreciated!

Ready-made resources

We have loads of resources to get you started as a volunteer, and you can always create and share your own. Find out more on the resources page.
